Setting: the nonlinear Perron eigenproblem of the Krasikov-Lagarias 3x+1 difference-inequality system. Level-k states: residues m == 2 (mod 3), taken mod 3^k. A positive vector c satisfies c = F(c) with
  F(c)(m) = lam^{-2} c(4m mod 3^k)
          + [m==2 mod 9] lam^{alpha-2} mu((4m-2)/3 mod 3^{k-1})
          + [m==8 mod 9] lam^{alpha-1} mu((2m-1)/3 mod 3^{k-1}),
where alpha = log2(3), and mu(r) = min over the three level-k lifts {r + j*3^{k-1}, j=0,1,2} of c. Here lam = lam_k in (1.84,1.88) for k=15..19 (the feasibility threshold, Perron value 1); lam <= 2 always; lam^{alpha-1} ~ 1.44 > 1 (advanced branch), lam^{alpha-2} ~ 0.77, lam^{-2} ~ 0.29 (transport).

Key arithmetic facts: -1 == 8 (mod 9) and R8(-1) = (2(-1)-1)/3 = -1, so at m = -1 the min term is over the fiber of -1 itself, giving the EXACT identity
  (I)  min_j c(-1 + j*3^{k-1}) = lam^{1-alpha} c(-1) - lam^{-1-alpha} c(-4).
Local ball B_nu = {-1 + u*3^{k-nu} mod 3^k : u mod 3^nu}: all its states are == 8 (mod 9) for nu <= k-2. In offset coordinates q = u/3^nu in the Pruefer group Z(3^infty) = Z[1/3]/Z, the branch-min at state q references the three "children" y with 3y = 2q (i.e. (2q+i)/3, i=0,1,2) one depth deeper, plus a transport term lam^{-2}c(4x) referencing the ball of -4 (relative size measured 4.5e-4 at the top of the window at k=18, decaying per level by factor ~0.67).

Pure-branch limit system on Z(3^infty):
  (P)  Pi(q) = lam^{alpha-1} * min{ Pi(y) : 3y = 2q },  Pi(0) = 1,  Pi > 0.
My results so far, which I want checked and extended:
(1) Writing Pi(q) = lam^{(1-alpha)nu(q)} * H(q) (nu(q) = denominator exponent of q), (P) says H(q) = min over children H(y): H is "min-harmonic" on the 3-ary tree of children (each q has children the three solutions of 3y=2q; every q has 0 as an iterated ancestor; 0 is its own child, a self-loop). The self-loop at 0 plus Pi(0)=1 forces min(Pi(1/3), Pi(2/3)) = lam^{1-alpha} EXACTLY, for every positive solution of (P). Empirically (certified extremal eigenvectors, k = 15..19): the fiber profile of -1 normalized by c(-1) is (1, a_k, b_k) up to the swap of the two nontrivial lifts each level (the labeled action across levels is exactly offset-doubling u -> 2u mod 3^nu, verified numerically to 0.3%); and a_k = lam^{1-alpha} - lam^{-1-alpha} c(-4)/c(-1) holds to 1e-6 (it is identity (I)).
